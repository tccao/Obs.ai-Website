About OBS.ai

Empowering businesses with AI-driven analytics solutions

Our Founder

Our founder brings extensive expertise in web development paired with an innate ability to develop market-ready products. With a proven track record of creating solutions utilized by Fortune 500 companies and generating over $1 million in combined revenue, they established OBS.ai with a vision to revolutionize how businesses leverage their data through artificial intelligence.

Their background in building scalable, enterprise-grade applications has been instrumental in developing OBS.ai's robust architecture that serves organizations of all sizes while maintaining the agility to adapt to evolving market needs.

Our Mission

At OBS.ai, we're dedicated to democratizing access to advanced data analytics through artificial intelligence. Our mission is to empower organizations to uncover actionable insights from their data, enabling smarter decision-making and business optimization across all operational levels.

We strive to bridge the gap between complex data science and practical business applications, creating tools that transform raw information into strategic advantage. Through our platform, we aim to make AI-powered analytics accessible, understandable, and valuable for businesses regardless of their technical expertise.

Our Principles

Challenge What Is Known

We approach challenges by breaking them down to their foundational truths rather than relying on analogies or established conventions. This allows us to develop truly innovative solutions that address root causes rather than symptoms.

Think Outside A Box

We believe in setting audacious goals that push the boundaries of what's possible in data analytics and AI. By aiming high, we continuously evolve our platform to deliver increasingly powerful capabilities to our users.

Move Quickly

We embrace an iterative approach that values rapid development and continuous improvement. Rather than getting caught in analysis paralysis, we prioritize action, learn from real-world implementation, and swiftly address issues as they arise.

Offices

Our headquarters is located in downtown Chicago, where our team of data scientists, developers, and business strategists collaborate to advance our platform's capabilities. Situated in this vibrant business hub, we maintain close connections with both the technology sector and the diverse industries we serve.

From our Chicago base, we support clients across the globe, helping them harness the power of their data through our innovative AI solutions.
